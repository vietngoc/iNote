iNotes version 0.1.5

Keep your thinks!

Usage:

- Install the extension and icon iNote will appear on Awesome Bar.
- Right-click the paragraphs that you want to save and choose "Save to new note".
- Click icon iNote on the Awesomebar.
- Yeah! The text you selected has been saved. :D
- You can also click to create a blank note and type what you are thinking. :)
